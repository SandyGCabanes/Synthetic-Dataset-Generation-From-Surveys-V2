### Summary:

- **normalize_text()** and **split_multi()** are basic utilities used inside **explode_multiselect()** to transform multi-select string columns into exploded long format.
- **explode_multiselect()** feeds into **get_freq_table()** and **compare_freqs()** for frequency calculation.
- **compare_freqs()** outputs feed into **generate_plotly_bar_chart()** for dashboards.
- **calculate_disclosure()** and **calculate_utility_diff()** are privacy and utility assessors called inside the **parameter tuning loop** to evaluate each synthetic dataset.
- The **parameter tuning loop** generates synthetic datasets and uses assessment functions to find the best parameter combination.
- Final outputs include reports from DataExplorer and interactive multi-select dashboards produced via the helper functions.
